How it works?

As this is the 1st version, there are no English lang. support so;

click the "CPU analyse.exe" twice.
then click "SİSTEMİ ANALİZ ET VE YARGILA" ("analyse the system and jugde")
After a few miliseconds, you see the results as a total score which analysises your system and then multiplies the cpu core number with cpu memory speed.
If its bigger than 12k>, you'll see a text that says "GÜÇLÜ BİR CANAVAR" means "A STRONG BEAST". That's the best score.
If its bigger than 9000 but lower than 12000 it will say "ORTA ŞEKERLİ" basically means "MID".
If its lower than 9000 it will say "DAWG (KİLLER DEVREDE)" means "DAWG (KILLER ACTIVATED)".
